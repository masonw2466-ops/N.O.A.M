Learn About Cybersecurity!

Cybersecurity is about protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. Here you'll find helpful quick tips on how to better protect your business.

Cybersecurity Basics

Keep software updated, back up your files, use passwords, and utilize Multi-Factor Authentication!

Smart Security Practices

Don't reuse passwords, limit log-in attempts, and train your staff regularly about new risks!

Physical Security

Lock up sensitive documents, limit access, track all devices that store data, shred sensitive documents, and wipe old devices before disposal!

Phishing Awareness

Always verify who is sending messages, send out simulated phishing attempts to test employees, and be sure to train and re-train employees who fail these tests!

Secure Wireless Access

Use WPA2/WPA3 routers, avoid public Wi-Fi unless you have a VPN, keep antivirus active and updated, and train staff on safe remote work practices!

Cyber Insurance

Be sure to purchase insurance coverage for both your and your clients' needs!
