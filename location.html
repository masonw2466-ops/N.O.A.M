Directions to Southern Utah University Student Center Parking Lot

From Main Street in Cedar City, turn east onto 200 South.
Continue straight until you reach the SUU campus.
Follow signs toward the Student Center.
The parking lot is located adjacent to the Student Center building.
Visitors may use designated guest parking spaces.
